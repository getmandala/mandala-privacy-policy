Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Short version: Your personal data never leaves your device. We have no server, we collect no analytics, and we cannot see anything you enter into the app.

Overview

Mandala is a personal goal-tracking app based on the Harada Method. We are committed to your privacy. This policy explains what data is stored, where it lives, and what we (don't) do with it.

Data Stored on Your Device

All data you enter — your name, goal, focus areas, actions, check-in history, streaks, XP, rank, earned badges, and settings — is stored exclusively on your device using local storage (AsyncStorage).

This data is never transmitted to any server. We have no backend, no database, and no way to access anything you enter.

In-App Purchases

Mandala offers an optional one-time Pro upgrade. Purchases are processed entirely by Apple (iOS) or Google (Android) through their native payment systems. Your payment details are never shared with us.

We use RevenueCat as a purchase management SDK solely to verify your entitlement on-device. RevenueCat may collect an anonymous app user ID and your purchase receipt for this purpose.

RevenueCat privacy policy: revenuecat.com/privacy

Notifications

If you enable daily reminders, notifications are scheduled entirely on your device using the operating system's local notification API. No data is transmitted off your device to deliver these reminders.

You can disable notifications at any time in your device Settings or inside the Mandala app.

Analytics & Tracking

We do not use any analytics SDKs, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies. We do not collect crash reports, usage statistics, or any personally identifiable information.

Data Deletion

You can permanently delete all your data at any time by using the "Reset All Data" option in the app's Settings screen. Uninstalling the app will also remove all locally stored data from your device.

Children

Mandala is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, we will notify you through an app update. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
